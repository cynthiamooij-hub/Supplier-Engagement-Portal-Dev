01 — Why we are asking

Most of our footprint sits with our suppliers, not inside our own plants.

71% of The Corporate's emissions are in Scope 3 — the goods, services, and logistics our suppliers provide. We cannot account for what we do not measure, and we cannot measure it without you.

We have committed to net-zero by 2045, with a 50% reduction against our 2023 base year by 2030. These targets depend on accurate data from across our value chain.

Sustainability reporting is changing. Like many companies, we are building CSRD readiness, and that requires visibility into supplier performance now rather than later.

This is the start of a working relationship, not a one-time request. We are asking for partnership, and we will share what we learn back with you.
